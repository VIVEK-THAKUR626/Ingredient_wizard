About Ingredients Wizard

Ingredients Wizard is a web app that helps users discover delicious recipes based on ingredients they already have. You can filter recipes by dietary preferences and cuisines, save your favorites, and explore healthy, convenient meal ideas.

Our goal is to reduce food waste, inspire creativity in cooking, and make meal planning easier for everyone.
